About Us

Local, Organic, Delicious. These are our requirements for every dish we prepare and we are proud to say that every ingredient we use is homegrown without any hormones.

The Team

Bon Appétit Catering is comprised of the Mother-Son duo, Jennifer and Jordan Teisan. They have both been cooking for years and their ability to make a mouth-watering meal is unparalled.

Goals

We only have one goal. We want to provide you and your event with a delectable meal that leaves you satisfied, yet not at the expense of your health.

Why our food is so good

Because we commit to our philosophy of natural, locally grown ingredients, we are able to offer our customers appetizing dishes that look as if they came from a 5-star restaurant. This restriction of ingredients doesn't come at the cost of our food, however. Every dish is prepared carefully in order to be as delicious as possible.

Why Bon Appétit?

Bon Appétit has been a family run/operated company since its inception nearly 30 years ago. We have been successful during those years due to our commitment to our friends and neighbors of the OC. We want to provide delicious meals to the community that has provided us with a place to call home for so long.
